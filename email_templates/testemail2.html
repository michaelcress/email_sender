Your Excellency:

I have the honor to write to you on behalf of the World Association of Former United Nations Internes and Fellows (WAFUNIF) , an organization established in 1978 with the support of the United Nations. Our mission is to strengthen the bonds among former and current UN interns and fellows, to promote international understanding, and to support the ideals and work of the United Nations.

As part of our ongoing efforts to rebuild and expand the WAFUNIF network, we are reaching out to Permanent Missions to kindly request your assistance in providing us with the contact information of your current interns. This will enable us to engage with them, offer networking opportunities, and ensure they remain connected to the UN family even after completing their assignments.

We deeply value the contributions that interns make to the work of the United Nations and believe that maintaining this global network is a meaningful way to support the next generation of leaders and advocates for multilateralism.
